Overture Maps bundled data notice
=================================

This application may bundle or cache Overture Maps datasets, including:

- Divisions (`theme=divisions`, `type=division_area`)
- Base infrastructure (`theme=base`, `type=infrastructure`)
- Places (`theme=places`, `type=place`)

Official attribution and licensing reference:
https://docs.overturemaps.org/attribution/

Required attribution shown by Overture for ODbL-backed themes:
© OpenStreetMap contributors, Overture Maps Foundation
